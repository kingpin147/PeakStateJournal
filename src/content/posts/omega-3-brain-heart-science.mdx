## EPA and DHA: Not All Omega-3s Are Equal

When most people think of omega-3s, they picture flaxseed or walnuts — plant sources rich in alpha-linolenic acid (ALA). However, ALA must be converted to EPA (eicosapentaenoic acid) and DHA (docosahexaenoic acid) to exert its biological effects. This conversion is extremely inefficient in humans — only 5–10% of ALA converts to EPA, and less than 1% converts to DHA.

EPA and DHA from marine sources — fatty fish, fish oil, or algae oil — bypass this conversion entirely and are the biologically active compounds responsible for omega-3's health benefits.

## The REDUCE-IT Trial

The REDUCE-IT trial published in the New England Journal of Medicine demonstrated that 4 grams of prescription-grade EPA daily reduced major adverse cardiovascular events by 25% in high-risk patients, with a 20% reduction in cardiovascular death. This is a larger effect size than most statin trials.

The mechanism: EPA incorporates into cell membrane phospholipids, reducing their fluidity in a way that stabilizes arterial plaques and reduces inflammatory signaling.

## Brain Structure and DHA

DHA constitutes approximately 30% of the fatty acid content of the brain's gray matter. It is a structural component of neuronal membranes and synapses. Chronically low DHA intake is associated with accelerated brain volume loss and increased Alzheimer's risk.

A randomized controlled trial published in Alzheimers & Dementia found that 900 mg DHA daily for 24 weeks significantly improved learning and memory in adults with mild cognitive decline compared to placebo.

## How to Optimize Your Omega-3 Intake

**Dietary sources:**
- Sockeye salmon (3.5 oz): ~1,800 mg EPA+DHA
- Atlantic sardines (3 oz): ~1,500 mg EPA+DHA
- Mackerel (3 oz): ~2,600 mg EPA+DHA

**Supplementation:**
- Target 2–4 grams of combined EPA+DHA daily
- Look for products tested for oxidation (rancid fish oil is pro-inflammatory)
- Algae-based omega-3 is equally effective for those avoiding fish
- Measure your Omega-3 Index (a blood test) — target above 8%
